The Wait...

59% of parents with children and young people with speech, language and communication needs have to fight to get support for their children's difficulties and more than half have to wait longer that six months to get it.
[icancharity.org.uk]

The Numbers...

It is estimated that 10% of children starting school have speech, language and communication needs
[afasic.org.uk]

The Impact...

Approximately 80% of children with emotional and behavioural disorders have significant language deficits
[RCSLT.org]

The Right Direction...

When language difficulties are resolved by the age of five and a half, students are more likely to go on to develop good reading and spelling skills
[thecommunicationtrust.org.uk]

The Little Speech Therapy Company is an independent speech and language therapy practice providing support to children in the South Norfolk and Norwich area.
The practice has been set up and led by Katherine Baxter, Specialist Speech and Language Therapist, who has over six years of experience working in both the NHS and independent therapy sectors.
After spending the past four years working in the NHS service for Norfolk, Katherine has observed first hand the difficulties that families and schools are facing in finding the correct support for their childâ€™s communication difficulties.
The Little Speech Therapy Company strives to be an approachable, flexible and affordable service, putting the needs of the child first.

Who do we see?


We are able to offer advice and support on children with:

  • Language delay/disorder
  • Phonological delay/disorder (speech sound)
  • Late talkers
  • Attention and Listening difficulties
  • Verbal Dyspraxia
  • Hearing Impairment
  • Dysfluency (stammering)
  • Learning difficulties
  • Global Developmental Delay
  • Social Communication Difficulties (and/or Autistic Spectrum Disorder)
  • Selective Mutism
